About Our Church

Welcome to WOPBIC

World of Prayer Bible International Church is a Bible-believing, Spirit-filled church committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ through fervent prayer, sound biblical teaching, and compassionate service to our community.

Our Mission

To equip believers for effective ministry through prayer, worship, and the study of God's Word, while reaching the lost with the message of salvation.

Our Vision

To be a lighthouse of hope and transformation, raising a generation of prayer warriors who will impact the world for Christ.

Core Values

  • Prayer & Intercession
  • Biblical Truth
  • Holy Spirit Power
  • Community Service
  • Global Missions

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our final authority for faith and practice.

Salvation

We believe salvation is found only through faith in Jesus Christ and His finished work on the cross.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the operation of spiritual gifts in the church today.

Prayer

We believe in the power of prayer and intercession to transform lives and communities.

The Church

We believe the church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, and serve together.

Great Commission

We believe in fulfilling the Great Commission to make disciples of all nations.

Our Leadership

Our church is led by dedicated servants of God who are committed to shepherding and equipping the body of Christ.

Senior Pastor

Leading the vision and spiritual direction of the church

Pastoral Team

Providing care and oversight for the congregation

Deacons & Elders

Serving and supporting the ministry of the church
